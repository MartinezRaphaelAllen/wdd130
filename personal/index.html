Welcome to my Mission Journey!

This website presents my mission journey through pictures. A trip down memory lane, I dedicate this page to my friends, family, and fellow missionaries.

May this bring back fond memories of our time together!

Some interesting facts about my mission:

  1. I am in the MTC batch 741
  2. I had a total of 11 companions.
  3. I trained 2 missionaries
  4. I was a district leader for 2 cycles.
  5. I was a zone leader for 4 cycles.
  6. I was an assistant to the mission President for 4 cycles.
  1. While training a new missionary, I took the IELTS and SAT exams.
  2. I got bit by a stray cat in the church building
  3. We had a tradition called "Big Mac Thursdays"
  4. Each companionship in our mission shared 1 phone
  5. Got the chance to eat exotic meat like snail, rabbit, and duck
  6. Despite being in a kapampangan speaking area for 6 months, I never picked up the language
