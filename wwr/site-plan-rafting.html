Overview

Purpose

To create a website that present information to potential customers. It will showcase the amenities and activities we offer with accompanying images. The site will also be an avenue for customers to book and pay for reservations. Lastly, there will be a function that will let customers read and leave reviews.

Audience

This website will be targeted towards: -outdoor enthusiasts that want to experience water activties. -Single adults looking for a good camping venue. -Nature lovers that want to go away from the busy city.
